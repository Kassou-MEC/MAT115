Synthetic division is a shortcut method for dividing polynomials when the divisor is in the form (x - c). It uses only the coefficients.

Steps:

Remainder Theorem

When a polynomial P(x) is divided by (x - c), the remainder is P(c).

Factor Theorem: (x - c) is a factor of P(x) if and only if P(c) = 0.

Factoring Polynomials

What is Factoring?

Factoring is the process of expressing a polynomial as a product of simpler polynomials. It's the reverse of multiplication.
